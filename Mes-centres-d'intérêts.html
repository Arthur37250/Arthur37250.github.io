My hobbies

Football

Football is my favourite hobby, I have been playing this sport since I was 4 years old. Today I try to practice this passion as much as possible but with school it is not always easy to practice it. I played this sport at a certain level, I played until the Regional 1 in the club of Chambray-les-Tours. This sport allows me to recharge my batteries and clear my mind. What I also like about this sport is the fact that I can travel, play against great clubs and keep good memories. This sport allows me to learn how to deal with pressure and make quick decisions.

The world of networks 

What I like in this world are the different sales markets that we find in the operators but in particular the one around cyber security. This interest comes from my parents who work for one of the leading operators in the world, Orange. The part that interests me the most is the cybersecurity market. What I like in this field is the fact that I have several objectives, which are, for example, to provide an efficient solution to a customer and to manage important contracts with companies. That's why I joined the IUT in Blois because my objective is to specialise in cybersecurity..
